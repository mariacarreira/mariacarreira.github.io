### SIREn (Subunit Inference from Real-space Ensembles)

A computational tool to systematically and quantitatively analyze heterogeneity in volume ensembles from cryo-EM. [Publication link](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39415986/).

### multiDRGN

A generative model leveraging the [cryoDRGN framework](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-020-01049-4) to jointly reconstruct and analyze heterogeneity across related cryo-EM datasets, including time-resolved datasets. [Publication link — abstract](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12585762/). Manuscript in preparation.

### cryoDRGN with noise injections

A new cryoDRGN framework in which Gaussian noise, sampled from $\mathcal{N}(0,1)$, is added to latent embeddings during the first few training epochs to mitigate minor artifacts in reconstructed density maps and improve the learned representation of heterogeneity. [Publication link](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.05.08.722747v1.full.pdf).
