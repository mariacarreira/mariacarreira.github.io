### Automated model-free analysis of cryo-EM volume ensembles with SIREn

### Leveraging generative modeling to analyze multiple related cryo-EM datasets

A generative model augmenting the [cryoDRGN framework](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-020-01049-4) to jointly reconstruct and analyze heterogeneity across related cryo-EM datasets, including time-resolved datasets. [Abstract](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12585762/). Manuscript in preparation.


</div>

<div class="project-entry" markdown="1">
  
### Heterogeneous reconstruction algorithms for cryoEM achieve limited particle classification accuracy on real benchmark datasets

A new cryoDRGN framework in which Gaussian noise is added to latent embeddings during the first few training epochs to mitigate minor artifacts in reconstructed density maps and improve the learned representation of heterogeneity. [Manuscript](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.05.08.722747v1.full.pdf).
