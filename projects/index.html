projects

Selected cool projects I have worked on!

SIREn (Subunit Inference from Real-space Ensembles)

A computational tool to systematically and quantitatively analyze heterogeneity in volume ensembles from cryo-EM. Publication link.

multiDRGN

A generative model leveraging the cryoDRGN framework to jointly reconstruct and analyze heterogeneity across related cryo-EM datasets, including time-resolved datasets. Publication link — abstract. Manuscript in preparation.

cryoDRGN with noise injections

A new cryoDRGN framework in which Gaussian noise, sampled from $\mathcal{N}(0,1)$, is added to latent embeddings during the first few training epochs to mitigate minor artifacts in reconstructed density maps and improve the learned representation of heterogeneity. Publication link.
